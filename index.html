Renaissance Hair

We're a beautiful, bright little hair salon located in the heart of Caloundra, on Bulcock street.
We offer all services from colouring, cutting, bridal & formal hair, perming, chemical straightening, facial waxing plus much more!


At Renaissance Hair we strive to be the innovators of unique individual creation. A great haircut and colour service can give your hair instant oomph, vitality and bounce. It can erase or emphasise your features. At Renaissance Hair your service is unique and is designed to suit the individual you.

Our aim is to work with you to create a hairstyle that you look and feel great wearing and that suits your lifestyle beyond the salon experience. We aim to send you home feeling beautiful in every way, time after time. Our colour specialists involve themselves in designing the ultimate colour for you. We take into consideration whether you want a high maintenance colour or just a gloss colour, With highly skilled and knowledgeable friendly staff providing an extensive hair and beauty service, quality products, warm atmosphere, hot coffee and central location, Renaissance Hair provides an enjoyable experience that you will want to tell your friends about. Be at Renaissance Hair and enjoy the experience.
